About Me

About Me

I was born and rasied in Hong Kong and moved to the States when I was 14. I grew up in all girl's catholic school. We had to wear uniform to school, we cannot eat or drink in the classroom. We have to ask for permission to go to the bathroom and even walking to the front of the classroom to toss your garbage in the trash bin needs permission.

My first day of school in the States was eye opening for me. Everyone dressed differently, some students had purple hair, some students were wearing their pajamas, and some students were wearing high heels. Every time I hear someone's heels clicking behind me, my first reaction was turn around and greet whoever is walking behind me. Since in my old school, only teachers can wear heels and we need to greet them properly, so it took me a while getting used to that.

I remember the first time I witness a fight in the school cafeteria was terrifying for me. All the students around the fight did not try to stop it. The all stood there, some even put a chair on the lunch table and stand on the chair to get a better view of the fight. Students did not stop fighting until school administrator and police officers intervene.

Crystal Game

About

This game is called the Crystal Collector. Before you start playing, click on the "Click here to start" button, a random number between 18 and 120 will be assigned to you. On the bottom of the screen, there are 4 crystals. Each crystals have a hidden number between 1 and 12 assiged to it. You will need to click on each crystal to add up to the random number that is assigned to you. If your total number exceed the random number you loss, if your total number equals to the random number, you win. To start a new game, you may click on the "Click here to start" button. A new random number will be assigned as well as the hidden numbers behind each crystals. Have fun!

TRIVIA GAME

About

Let's find out how much do you know about songs in Disney movies by completing this trivia game. Before you start the game, click the start button to start the timer. You have 60 seconds to complete 8 questions. Each questions has 4 choices to choose from. Once you are done, click the submit button, your score will be displayed on the screen with numbers of questions you have answered correctly and number of questions that you missed. If you did not complete the entire game in 60 seconds, the timer and game will stop and your score will be displayed with the number of questions you answered correctly, number of questions you missed, and number of questions you did not answer. Have fun and good luck!

TRAIN SCHEDULER

About

This train scheduler helps you track when's the next train coming and how many minutes left for the next train to arrive. You simply need to enter the train name, destination, the first train time and frequency. The site will then calculate the next train's arrival time and waiting time for you based on the current time.

Liri-node-app

About

This is an app that will help you see all my tweets in my Twitter account, search movie information from the OMDB API, seach song's information you are listing to on Spotify NPM, and let liri do its thing. First, you need to type "node liri.js" and a space before each command below. If you would like to see my tweets, simply just type "my-tweets" to see them, Type "spotify-this-song" and the name of the song you want to search information on, Or type "movie-this" and the name of the movie you want more information on, Or type "do-what-it-says" and let liri do its thing. Have fun searching!
